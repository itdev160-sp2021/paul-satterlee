Cutting Down a Tree: A Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Find a Tree that needs to Die

By and large, trees are great. However some are a nuisance to people, places and things. These troubled trees are the ones you're going to want to cut down. At this point, don't worry about bombing a tree onto a house or a business, you're just going to want to kill this tree as fast as possible.

Step 2: Fire up your chainsaw

Don't worry about people around you and if they have any ear protection. Just fire that bad boy up and tell everyone to get the hell out of your way.

Step 3: Just Start Cutting

Just remember, it's everyone else's responsibility to watch out for you, not your responsibility to look out for them. If someone wants to stand under a tree as it's falling to the ground, then it was just their time.

To Recap...

  1. Find a Tree
  2. Start Chainsaw
  3. Cut it down and repeat
